About

Hello, my name is Rene Villegas (self-proclaimed data nerd extraordinaire) and I am a data analyst with 15+ years in management, training, recruiting, and hiring experience. In my previous roles, I established myself to be the "go-to" person in my department(s) concerning reporting, data mining, and data analytics. As a result, I successfully led initiatives to redesign scorecards/dashboards to improve employee satisfaction, created/redesigned call quality monitoring forms, and created/improved upon language specific standard operating procedures (SOPs). I have supported teams of 25 - 120 employees who concentrated on tech support, problem-solving, customer retention, and loyalty.

I am a passionate people-person who finds reward in mentoring, coaching, guiding, and helping others achieve professional success.
